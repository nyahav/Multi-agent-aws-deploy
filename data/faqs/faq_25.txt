Q: What is cyberPolice and what services do they provide?
A: cyberPolice is a fictional cybersecurity company specializing in threat detection and response for small and medium-sized businesses. cyberPolice offers 24/7 managed detection and response (MDR), real-time threat intelligence, rapid incident response and containment, vulnerability assessments, penetration testing, security awareness training, and compliance support (ISO/PCI/GDPR). They combine machine-learning-based detection with a human-led SOC (security operations center) to minimize dwell time and restore operations quickly.
